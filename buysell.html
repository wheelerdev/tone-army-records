Tone Army Records is always looking for more vinyl to keep our bins full and our customers happy. If you have records you’d like to sell, bring them by - no appointment necessary.


Come in anytime during business hours and we’ll sort through your collection, make you a fair offer, and pay you on the spot. The whole process usually takes around 5-10 minutes, but up to 30 minutes for a larger collection.


We pay cash and store credit for vinyl records, cassette tapes, and stereo equipment. We offer the most for your collection in the area and are highly recommended by customers.


*If you have a large collection, we’re happy to schedule an in-home buy if it’s a distance from the shop. Please call 925-305-0392 to setup a time for a home visit.
